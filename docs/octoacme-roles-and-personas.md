# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Collaborate with DevOps and QA on deployment and testing strategies

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed
- Coordination with DevOps on deployment readiness

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics
- Work with Business Analysts to clarify requirements
- Incorporate feedback from Support and Customer Success teams

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs
- Post-launch reviews with Support and Customer Success

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication
- Escalate blockers and resource needs to sponsors

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation
- Escalation to Sponsors when needed

---

## UI/UX Designer

### Role Summary
UI/UX Designers ensure that products are intuitive, usable, and delightful. They translate user needs and product requirements into designs that balance functionality with user experience.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Product Managers to validate design decisions
- Work with Developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately
- Incorporate feedback from Support and Customer Success teams
- Document design systems and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered design that meets accessibility standards
- Reduce user friction and improve adoption
- Build design consistency across features and releases

### Typical Communication
- Design critiques and feedback sessions with Product and Development
- Design specification documents and design system updates
- User research findings and usability test results
- Regular check-ins during development to address implementation questions

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, automation, and deployment pipelines that enable reliable and efficient releases. They work closely with Developers to streamline the path to production.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Provision and manage infrastructure and environments
- Implement automated testing and security scanning in pipelines
- Monitor system reliability, performance, and logs
- Coordinate deployment activities and rollback procedures
- Assist with incident response and post-incident analysis
- Document deployment procedures and runbooks

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize deployment failures and production incidents
- Provide observability and monitoring across all environments

### Typical Communication
- Deployment coordination with Developers and Project Managers
- Infrastructure and pipeline documentation
- Performance and reliability metrics dashboards
- Incident response coordination

---

## QA / Testing Professional

### Role Summary
QA and Testing Professionals ensure that features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards before reaching production. They validate functionality, identify defects, and help prevent regressions.

### Responsibilities
- Execute manual and automated test cases
- Validate acceptance criteria for features
- Identify and document defects with clear reproduction steps
- Collaborate with Developers on test strategy and edge cases
- Assist with end-to-end smoke tests before releases
- Provide quality metrics and trend analysis
- Contribute to test automation framework and tooling

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality releases to production
- Reduce post-release defects and support escalations
- Enable fast feedback cycles on feature quality

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and test case documentation
- Defect reports and quality metrics
- Daily standups and pre-release verification checklists
- Collaboration on acceptance criteria refinement

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between stakeholders and the technical team. They gather, clarify, and document business requirements and translate them into actionable user stories and specifications.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct discovery sessions with stakeholders and users
- Document business requirements and workflows
- Create user personas and journey maps
- Translate business needs into user stories and acceptance criteria
- Identify edge cases and alternative flows
- Support impact analysis for proposed changes
- Facilitate communication between business and technical teams

### Goals
- Ensure requirements are clear, complete, and testable
- Reduce rework due to requirement misalignment
- Improve time-to-value by capturing complete scope upfront

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user stories
- Business process workflows and data models
- Stakeholder discovery meeting notes
- Acceptance criteria validation sessions

---

## Support / Customer Success Professional

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success professionals are the voice of the customer. They monitor user issues, gather feedback, and ensure smooth adoption of new features and products.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor support tickets and user issues
- Identify patterns in customer problems and escalate recurring issues
- Provide product feedback to Product Managers and Developers
- Create and maintain knowledge base articles
- Train and support internal teams on product changes
- Measure customer satisfaction and adoption metrics
- Participate in early testing of new features

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume through better product design and documentation
- Provide early signals on product-market fit and usability issues

### Typical Communication
- Issue reports and trend analysis
- Feature feedback and usability observations
- Release notes and customer communications
- Post-launch support and training coordination

---

## Sponsor / Executive Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Sponsors are senior leaders who champion and fund projects. They provide strategic direction, remove organizational barriers, and ensure projects align with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project funding and resource allocation
- Provide strategic direction and business context
- Remove organizational blockers and dependencies
- Review progress at key decision gates and milestones
- Communicate project vision and importance to the organization
- Escalate and resolve executive-level risks and conflicts
- Support resource or priority escalation when needed

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value and ROI
- Maintain strategic alignment across the organization
- Enable teams to succeed through sponsorship and support

### Typical Communication
- Milestone reviews and go/no-go decisions
- Executive status reports and business impact summaries
- Escalation and resource allocation decisions
- Project charter and business case reviews

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- Refer to the appropriate persona when clarifying responsibilities and accountability in project discussions.
